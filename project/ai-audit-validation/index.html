AI‑Powered Audit Validation Engine

Client: Global retail brand Year: 2024 Role: AI/ML Engineer

Transaction‑level validation engine built with Python and LLMs, improving audit accuracy from 60% → 90% and preventing more than £500K in annual revenue leakage by automatically flagging inconsistencies and risks.

Context

The client managed thousands of daily transactions across multiple channels, with manual sampling and spreadsheet‑based checks leading to missed anomalies, slow audit cycles, and revenue leakage. Audit teams needed a way to automatically surface inconsistent data, suspicious patterns, and non‑compliant records without increasing headcount.

Approach

I designed an AI‑driven validation engine that combines deterministic checks with LLM‑based reasoning. Each transaction is enriched with context, evaluated against a library of rules, and then passed through LLM prompts that assess description, amounts, and metadata for inconsistencies and potential violations.

  • Ingested POS and order data into a structured validation pipeline.
  • Implemented rule‑based checks for hard constraints (formats, ranges, mandatory fields).
  • Used LLMs to analyse free‑text descriptions and patterns that were hard to capture in rules.
  • Prioritised and labeled issues to focus human reviewers on the highest‑risk items.

Impact

  • Audit accuracy improved from 60% → 90% on historical test sets.
  • Estimated £500K+ in yearly revenue protected by catching leakage earlier in the flow.
  • Manual review effort reduced by around 30% through prioritised issue queues.
  • Audit and finance teams gained clearer explanations for each flagged anomaly.

Responsibilities

End‑to‑end design and implementation of the validation engine, from data modelling and prompt design to deployment and dashboard handover for business stakeholders.

Technologies

Python LLMs (GPT‑4, Gemini, Claude) Pandas SQL Dashboards

What this illustrates

How I combine classical validation logic with LLM reasoning to create practical systems that fit into existing audit workflows and deliver measurable business impact.
